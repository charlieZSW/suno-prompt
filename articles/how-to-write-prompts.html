How to Write Effective Suno AI Prompts?

Published on 2025-06-25

The Importance of Prompts

Prompts are the bridge between you and Suno AI. A good prompt can accurately convey your creative intentions and guide the AI to generate music that meets your expectations. The quality of prompts directly affects the quality of generated music, so mastering the skills of writing high-quality prompts is crucial.

Core Elements of an Effective Prompt

An effective prompt usually includes the following elements:

  • Music Style (Genre): Clearly tell the AI what type of music you want, such as "Pop", "Rock", "Jazz", "Classical", etc. You can also try more specific sub-styles, such as "Synthwave Pop" or "Acoustic Folk".
  • Mood & Atmosphere: Describe the emotions and atmosphere you want the music to convey. For example, "upbeat, energetic", "sad, melancholic", "epic, cinematic".
  • Instruments: Specify the instruments you want to hear in the music. For example, "acoustic guitar, piano, violin", "808 drums, synthesizer, bass".
  • Tempo & Rhythm: Describe the tempo and rhythm of the music. For example, "slow tempo", "fast-paced, driving rhythm".
  • Vocals: If you need vocals, you can describe the type and style of vocals. For example, "female vocalist, soulful", "male rapper, aggressive".

Prompt Examples

Example 1: A catchy pop song

Pop, upbeat, energetic, catchy chorus, female vocalist, synthesizer, drum machine, bassline, 120 BPM

Example 2: A cinematic epic soundtrack

Epic, cinematic, orchestral, powerful brass section, soaring strings, choir, dramatic percussion, slow build-up

Example 3: A relaxing folk song

Acoustic folk, calm, relaxing, male vocalist, acoustic guitar, gentle piano, harmonica solo, fingerpicking style
